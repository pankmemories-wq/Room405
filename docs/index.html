Do's & Don'ts
1

Step out for phone calls

Take calls in the kitchen or balcony. If you must talk in the room, keep your voice low.

2

Respect sleeping hours

Silent or vibrate mode near a sleeping roommate. No loud talk, laughter, or noise nearby.

3

No loudspeaker, ever

Use earphones for calls, music, and videos. Lowest comfortable volume if none available.

4

Clean up after yourself

No dirty dishes left overnight. Washing duty covers cooking utensils, not personal dishes.

5

Save electricity

Switch off lights, fans, and chargers when not needed. Last one out checks everything's off.

6

Split groceries fairly

Shared items split equally among all 4. Track expenses, settle dues every week.

7

Lock up, always

Lock the door even for short trips out. No sharing the room key or code without group consent.

8

Talk it out

Raise issues directly and calmly. No silent treatment. Bigger matters go to a group discussion.

9

Lights-out policy

Agree on a weeknight lights-out time. Staying up late? Use a personal lamp, keep alarms soft.

Golden Principles
  1. Do your night duty on time — swap in advance if needed.
  2. Step out to talk on the phone — always.
  3. Silent phones when someone is sleeping.
  4. Earphones first, always.
  5. Clean as you go — leave no mess behind.
  6. Split expenses fairly and settle weekly.
  7. Talk it out — no grudges in Room 405.
